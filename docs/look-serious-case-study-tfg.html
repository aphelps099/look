Tech Futures Group

From scattered initiatives to unified platform

Tech Futures Group is a venture platform connecting investors with emerging technology opportunities across multiple sectors—AI, climate tech, biotech, and more. They had built something impressive: a network of investors, a portfolio of promising companies, and a reputation for spotting winners early.

The problem? Their brand had grown organically across four separate initiatives, each with its own look, voice, and website. To outsiders, it looked like four different companies. The lack of cohesion was undermining their credibility—exactly the opposite of what a venture platform needs.

The Challenge

Four brands that needed to become one.

Each of TFG's initiatives had developed its own identity over time. Different logos, different color palettes, different messaging approaches. What started as flexibility had become fragmentation.

Investors visiting one initiative had no idea it was connected to the others. Partners were confused about what TFG actually was. The team spent hours explaining relationships that should have been obvious at a glance.

They didn't need a refresh. They needed a unification—a system that could contain multiple initiatives while clearly communicating they were part of something bigger.

Before: Initiative 1
Before: Initiative 2

The Solution

A flexible system with a unified core.

We started with positioning: what is Tech Futures Group at its core? Not a collection of initiatives—a platform. A single entity with multiple expressions. That shift in framing changed everything.

The visual system we built has a strong parent brand with room for sub-brands to breathe. Shared typography and grid. Consistent voice. A color system that allows variation while maintaining family resemblance.

The website brings it all together—a single destination that makes the platform legible while giving each initiative its own space. Investors can now see the full picture in seconds.

The Result

One platform, clearly understood.

The new system launched across all initiatives simultaneously. For the first time, Tech Futures Group looked like what it was: a serious platform with serious capabilities.

The team stopped explaining and started doing. Investors immediately understood the scope. Partners saw the full picture. And TFG had a foundation they could build on for years.
