You get a new logo, maybe some colors, a website that looks good for a year. But nothing's documented. Nothing scales. The moment you need to create something new, you're guessing—or paying someone to guess for you.

The BOS™ is different. It's the complete operating system for your brand—strategy, identity, and implementation, all documented in one place. So your brand works the same whether you're there or not.
