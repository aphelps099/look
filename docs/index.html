01 Venture Platform

Tech Futures Group

The Gap

A venture platform backed by state and federal funding, advising NorCal's most promising tech startups—but presenting like a regional nonprofit. The brand didn't match the caliber of companies they were accelerating.

The System

Strategic repositioning as a "venture platform" (not just SBDC program). Custom website with startup-grade design language. Messaging architecture that speaks to ambitious founders, not grant compliance.

The Result

Brand now attracts the founders it deserves. "Move Faster. Build Bigger." became a rallying cry that matches the ambition of their portfolio companies.

02 Climate Tech / Infrastructure

NeoPave

The Gap

Revolutionary road technology—polymer pavement that lasts 2-3x longer than asphalt, endorsed by LA, adopted by Apple and Google. But the brand felt like a science project, not a $3.7 trillion infrastructure play.

The System

Investor-grade visual identity and website. Messaging reframe from "sustainable alternative" to "the 80-year-old road industry just met its successor." Data architecture that tells the ROI story at scale.

The Result

Brand now commands the room in investor conversations. The technology speaks for itself—now so does the presentation.

03 Premium Fitness

Forever Young Fitness

The Gap

A trainer with 30+ years of elite experience—trusted by gym owners and competitive athletes—running a premium personal training service out of a Squarespace template. The expertise was world-class; the brand looked like a side hustle.

The System

Complete rebrand from "Zeb's Platinum Fitness" to "Forever Young Fitness"—a name that promises the outcome, not just the service. Premium visual identity that matches the caliber of training. Digital presence built for conversion.

The Result

Brand now commands premium pricing with confidence. Clients see the expertise before they ever meet the trainer—exactly how it should be.
