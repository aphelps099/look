About

I build brands that work as hard as you do.

I'm Aaron—a brand strategist, designer, and builder. I started Look Serious because I kept seeing the same problem: great companies with forgettable brands. Businesses that had outgrown their first logo. Teams doing exceptional work behind websites that could belong to anyone.

I've spent years at the intersection of strategy and execution—not just thinking about brands, but building them. Positioning. Identity. Websites. Systems. The whole thing.

What I've learned: a logo isn't a brand. A website isn't a brand. A brand is a system—the way everything works together to make you unmistakable. That's what I build.

I work with a handful of clients each quarter, by design. Not because I'm trying to seem exclusive—because this work requires focus. Deep attention. The kind of thinking that doesn't happen when you're juggling fifteen projects.

What I believe

Your brand is either an asset or a liability. There is no neutral. Every day it's either opening doors or closing them.

Strategy without execution is philosophy. I'm not here to hand you a PDF and wish you luck. I build the thing.

The best brands don't explain what they do. They demonstrate why they win.

AI changes everything—if you use it right. It's not a replacement for strategy. It's an accelerant. I help you find the right things to accelerate.

Good fit / Not a fit

We'll work well together if:

  • You've outgrown your current brand
  • You want a partner, not a vendor
  • You trust experts to do expert work
  • You're ready to invest in positioning
  • You think long-term

Probably not a fit if:

  • You just need a quick logo
  • You're shopping for the lowest price
  • Decisions require a committee
  • You need it done yesterday
  • You want to approve every pixel

If this sounds like the way you like to work, I'd enjoy meeting you. No pitch, no pressure—just a conversation to see if there's a gap worth closing together.
