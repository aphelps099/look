How we help Tough Tech founders

  1. Long-term Capital

    The Engine Fund prioritizes breakthrough ideas over early returns by providing long-term capital to startups that need time to make an impact.

  2. Labs, Equipment, Tools & Space

    The Engine Infrastructure provides access to specialized equipment and labs, both on-site and through a network of partner facilities, giving portfolio companies the tools and space necessary to work efficiently and economically.

  3. Connections

    The Engine Network facilitates the creation of long-term mutually beneficial relationships between founders, startups, corporates, government, strategic partners, among others.
